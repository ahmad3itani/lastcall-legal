# LAST CALL - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

LAST CALL is a pass-and-play party game that runs entirely on your device.

## We do not collect your data

This version of LAST CALL does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or device data. The game works fully offline. Your teams, settings, and custom decks are stored only on your device and are never sent to us or anyone else.

## No accounts, no ads, no tracking

There is no sign-in or account, no advertising, and no analytics, cookies, or tracking identifiers of any kind.

## Purchases

Optional premium packs are sold and processed by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment details. Only Google Play handles the transaction.

## What we never access

We do not access your location, contacts, photos, microphone, or advertising identifiers, and we do not sell data.

## Age

LAST CALL is intended for adults of legal drinking age, 21 and over, and is not directed to children.

## Changes and contact

If a future version adds optional features that collect data such as account sign-in, this policy and our Data safety disclosure will be updated first.
